“Who We Are”

We are launching two new marketplaces (FINRA/SEC Regulated ATS) for trading stocks.
Our first marketplace will utilize our patented Hide Side order type. This will allow investors to publicly advertise blocks openly without the fear of being gamed.
Our second marketplace will introduce the concept that size and price can be negotiated simultaneously. Currently markets can only electronically negotiate price.

Our Proposition to the Market

“How many more shares beyond what is offered at the NBBO can I get filled if I pay an extra penny towards the execution of my order.”
Market makers will determine this through a competitive “Size Auction.” Nowhere in the world does this form of auction exist.
We plan to launch our marketplaces in the coming months.
