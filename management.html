Our Team Members

Sam Balabon

Age 51

Chief Executive Officer and President and Chairman of the Board.

Mr. Balabon is a serial entrepreneur, money manager, trader and inventor with multiple patents. He serves as the chief compliance officer of our broker-dealer subsidiary and holds all of the appropriate securities licenses.

James O’Reilly

Age 51

Director, Chief Marketing Officer

Mr. O’Reilly brings 20 years of experience in developing, managing and implementing electronic trading platforms. He was the COO of Global Access Holdings, and before that held a senior management position at Sanford Bernstein & Company. Prior to that, Mr. O’Reilly held a number of positions at Charles Schwab before becoming a senior trader in the equities division. During his tenure at Schwab he played an active role in the development of the firm’s electronic trading platforms and technology.

Craig Kravetz

Age 45

Head of Customer Onboarding

Mr. Kravetz has over twenty years of experience in the deployment of high performance trading platforms at firms such as Nasdaq. These systems continue to be widely used throughout the financial industry. Over his career he has lead initiatives in high speed, order management, and quantitative platforms.

Prakash Patel

Age 54

Director

Mr. Patel is major shareholder and lender to our Company.He is currently the President/CEO of MR Enterprise of NY Inc., as well as Vice President of Hemisphere Management, an asset management and ownership company with a portfolio of hotels, restaurants and investment properties. Addition-ally, Mr. Patel serves on the Chairman of the Board of directors of Hanover Community Bank in Garden City Park, NY, a position appointed since the bank’s inception.

Kamran Rafieyan


Advisor

Mr. Rafieyan co-founded Lava Trading in 1999 as its Chief Information Officer . Prior to Lava, he held technology posts or consulted with a number of leading firms,including GFI Group, Market Arts Software, Bellcore, Reuters, Nomura Securities and Dow Jones/Telerate. He is a summa cum laude graduate of Princeton University with a B.S.E. in Electrical Engineering in 1989. He earned his M.S. with honors from the University of California at Berkeley.

Chris Nagy


Advisor

Mr. Nagy is a 25-year veteran of Wall Street, was previously the Managing Director of Order Routing, Sales and Strategy at TD Ameritrade for 12 years.
